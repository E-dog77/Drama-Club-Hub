Session Flow

Each Drama Club session should follow a clear structure to keep things engaging, safe, and purposeful. Here's the recommended flow:

  1. Warmup: Start with a game or activity that energises and connects the group.
  2. Main Activity: The core part of the session where major skills are developed.
  3. Main Activity 2 (Optional): A follow-up or deeper extension of the main task.
  4. Cool Down: End with a relaxing or reflective task to settle and close the session.
