Drama Club Sessions

Best Warmups

  • Pass the Energy Ball: Activates body, voice, and imagination. Low pressure, great for focus.
  • Honey, I Love You: Fun and social. Builds emotional control and confidence.
  • Clap Your Hands Game: Builds awareness, group rhythm, and focus quickly.
  • What Are You Doing?: Boosts fast thinking and gets everyone physically involved.
  • Energy Circle: Gentle collaboration. Fosters group connection and expressive warmup.
  • Dramatic Fish: Light and silly—builds up group energy at the start.

Best Main Activities

  • Change!: High-energy improvisation. Builds adaptability and teamwork.
  • Freeze Tag: Improvisation and spatial awareness. Keeps things fun and unpredictable.
  • Death in a Minute: Fast, funny scene work with character and creativity challenges.
  • 321: Boosts spontaneity, ensemble trust, and confidence.
  • Yes, And...: Teaches collaboration and the core of all improv—building ideas together.
  • One Word Per Person Story: Strengthens timing, articulation, and listening.
  • Open Scripts: Great for building vocal skills and characterisation in scripted work.
  • Andy’s Coming: Fun, energetic and great prep for physical activities.

Best Cool Downs

  • Mirror: Calming partner work. Builds trust and helps settle energy.
  • Honey, I Love You: Social and light-hearted way to close a session.
  • Energy Circle: Easy group activity to restore focus and lower intensity.
  • Clap Your Hands Game: Rhythmic bonding that brings calm and awareness.
  • Pass the Energy Ball (Gently): Soothing version of the warmup that grounds energy and attention.
