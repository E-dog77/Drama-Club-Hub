Drama Club Safety Guidelines

Physical Safety Guidelines

Clear the Space: Remove obstacles before starting. No running unless it’s part of the game.

Safe Movement: Warm up first. No lifting others. Respect physical limits.

Hydration & Breaks: Encourage water breaks. Allow participants to opt out when needed.

Emotional Safety Considerations

Opt-In Culture: Participation is encouraged but never forced.

Constructive Feedback Only: No mocking or negative comments.

Safe Topics: Avoid triggering content unless supported and prepared.

No Excuses: Discriminatory behaviour isn’t okay “in character.”

Activity-Specific Precautions

Activity Precautions
Freeze Tag Watch for collisions. Avoid jumping or tackling.
Mirror Game Emphasize slow, careful movement. Eye contact over speed.
Puppets Movers should guide gently. Communicate clearly before touching joints.

Leadership Response Protocols

Injury: Pause, check in, and report to staff (e.g., LHI, CEM). Document the incident.

Emotional Distress: Offer space and privacy. Let them speak when ready. Alert a trusted adult.

Unsafe Behaviour: Intervene immediately. Discuss privately. Log and escalate if repeated.

Consent Issues: Always check before physical/emotional scenes. Stop at the first sign of discomfort.
